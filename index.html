Short Bio

I am a 9th-grade student at Mary Institute and Saint Louis Country Day School (MICDS). I believe in the importance of healthy competition—pushing myself to be better without losing sight of collaboration and respect.

My academic interests span across Mathematics, Physics, and World History. Outside of the classroom, I enjoy leadership roles where I can help organize events and encourage others to find joy in STEM subjects.
